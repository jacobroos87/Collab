Our Process

Design Process

Whether it’s a start-up or an existing site that needs a refresh our team is well equipped to deliver a bespoke solution.

We follow a phased approach to deliver your project. Firstly, we do extensive market research analysing competitors in your market to ensure your site will have a competitive edge. Our team will brainstorm with you to decide on the flow of the site and what content you have or will need for your project. Once this is decided we’d recommend a framework that we feel would best suit your needs.

In the next phase, our developers will create wireframes detailing the layout of the site on all devices opening the door to more collaboration and feedback. All website functionality will be discussed, ideas shared and the backend operation of the site finalized.

When the design is agreed upon we begin coding your site ensuring that the goals and objectives developed in the early stages are met. In the final phase, a series of tests are undertaken to further improve site performance.
